We consider OS sytem requirements, screen sizes, device specs, dimensions and many other aspects when designing a product for humans. However, we rarely think about people with disabilities.

For instance, 285 million people are estimated to be visually impaired worldwide: 39 million are blind and 246 have low vision. This alarm application I designed was inspired by them, by those who need a benefit from modern technologies just like any of us.
